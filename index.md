## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**What if the device does not show up in the software?**
Check your USB cable. Use the original cable if possible. Ensure that you installed the Samsung USB drivers properly on your Windows machine. Try using a different USB port on your computer, ideally a port on the back if you use a desktop PC.

**Does this tool work on Windows 7?**
The 2026 version prioritizes stability on Windows 10 and Windows 11. It might not function correctly on older operating systems.

**Will my data stay on the phone?**
Flashing firmware often clears the internal storage. Back up your important files, photos, and contacts before you begin.

**What does the "F. Reset Time" option do?**
This option resets the binary flash counter on the device. Keep it checked unless you have a specific reason to change it.

**Can I stop the process after I click Start?**
Stopping the process mid-way might cause errors. The software is designed to finish the full package once it begins.
